# HUNTER GATHERER

## A TRICK Game Mode

### Version 0.1

---

# Purpose

Hunter Gatherer is a resource-acquisition game built on top of the TRICK framework.

Unlike earlier TRICK games that focus primarily on truth, deception, trust, and interpretation, Hunter Gatherer focuses on:

> acquiring, protecting, and converting resources for survival and advancement.

Players are not attempting merely to determine what is true.

Players are attempting to survive and prosper while navigating a world of uncertain information.

---

# Core Premise

Every player represents an agent attempting to maximize:

* Time (τ)
* Money (M)
* Health (H)
* Trust (T)

while interacting with other agents through communication.

The default objective is:


# Core Actions

Players may:

* Make statements
* Ask questions
* Parry (challenge)
* Negotiate
* Trade
* Cooperate
* Compete
* Deceive
* Investigate

---

# Resource Acquisition

Resources may be gained through:

* successful trade
* successful persuasion
* reputation building
* cooperation
* discovery
* prediction
* negotiation

---

# Resource Loss

Resources may be lost through:

* deception exposure
* failed trades
* poor decisions
* misinformation
* conflict
* time pressure

---

# Victory Conditions

## Survival Mode

Remain operational:

```text
H = 1
```

for a defined number of rounds.

---

## Wealth Mode

Maximize:

```text
M
```

---

## Reputation Mode

Maximize:

```text
T
```

---

## Tribe Mode

A team's combined:

```text
τ + M + H + T
```

determines victory.

# Relationship to Other TRICK Games

Hunter Gatherer acts as a higher-level simulation.

Games such as:

* Truth
* Mirror
* Paradox
* Praxis

can occur within Hunter Gatherer as sub-games.

---

# Core Insight

Hunter Gatherer shifts the focus from:

> "Is this statement true?"

to:

> "What happens if people believe it?"

The game therefore models how information influences survival, cooperation, competition, and resource acquisition.

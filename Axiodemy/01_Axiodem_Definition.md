# 1. Birth of the Axiodem Concept

- Proposed **Axiodem** as the smallest **contextually indivisible** unit of meaning.
- Distinguished symbols from meanings.
- Key evolution: from 'smallest unit of meaning' to 'smallest unit of meaning within a context'.

## Progression
1. Atomic meaning.
2. Shared across languages.
3. Symbols encode axiodems; they do not contain meaning.
